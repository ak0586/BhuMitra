Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following personal information from you:

  • Name: Your full name for account identification and personalization
  • Email Address: Your email for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • Phone Number: Your contact number for account management and security purposes
  • Physical Address: Your address to help manage land records and property information
  • Location Data: Both approximate and precise location information

Location Information

We collect and use location data in the following ways:

  • Precise Location (GPS): Used to accurately measure land area and boundaries
  • Approximate Location: Used for general geographic features and mapping
  • Location Purpose: This data enables core functionality of the App, including land measurement, boundary mapping, and location visualization

Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information, including:

  • Device ID: Unique identifier for your device
  • Device Model: Information about your device hardware
  • Operating System: Version of Android you're using
  • App Usage Data: How you interact with the App

Communication
